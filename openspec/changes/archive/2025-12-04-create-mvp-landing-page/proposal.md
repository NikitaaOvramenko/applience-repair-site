# Change: Create MVP Landing Page

## Why
The business needs a high-performance, minimalist digital presence to capture appliance repair leads in the Toronto/GTA area. The goal is a "Digital Postcard" that loads instantly and converts visitors into calls.

## What Changes
- **New Capability**: `landing-page`
- **Architecture**: Establishing a standard directory structure (Components, Pages, Assets, Styles).
- **Design**: Implementing a specific minimalist design system (Blue/White/Gray).
- **Content**: Creating Home and Services sections with specific appliance repair offerings.

## Impact
- **Frontend**:
    - New components and pages in `applience-repair-frontend/src`.
    - Tailwind configuration updates.
    - Asset additions.
