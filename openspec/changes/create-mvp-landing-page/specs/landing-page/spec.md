## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Landing Page Structure

The system SHALL provide a responsive landing page optimized for mobile conversion.

#### Scenario: Hero Section

- **WHEN** a user visits the homepage
- **THEN** they see a Hero section with:
  - Headline: "Appliance Repair in Toronto & GTA"
  - List of services: Washer, Fridge, Dryer, Dishwasher, Stove & Oven
  - "Call Now" button (tel: link)
  - "More Info" button

#### Scenario: Services and Details

- **WHEN** the user scrolls to the services section
- **THEN** they see detailed service listings and "Service Areas" (Toronto, Vaughan, North York, etc.)

#### Scenario: Trust Signals

- **WHEN** viewing the details section
- **THEN** key benefits are displayed: "Service within 1â€“2 hours", "25+ years experience", "Certified technicians", "Warranty".

#### Scenario: Contact Actions

- **WHEN** a user wants to contact the business
- **THEN** they have access to WhatsApp, SMS, and Phone Call buttons.

### Requirement: Performance and Design

The system MUST adhere to strict performance and design constraints.

#### Scenario: Load Time

- **WHEN** the site is loaded on a mobile device (4G network equivalent)
- **THEN** it becomes interactive in less than 2 seconds.

#### Scenario: Design Aesthetic

- **WHEN** viewing the site
- **THEN** it follows the minimalist "Technical" theme:
  - Primary Color: Blue #005BBB
  - Backgrounds: White/Gray
  - Typography: Sans-serif (Montserrat/Roboto/Inter)
